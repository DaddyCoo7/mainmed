## Recommendations for Remaining Pages

### Formula for Unique Descriptions

**Title Structure:**
`[Specialty] Billing | [Unique Differentiator] | [Optional Metric]`

**Description Structure:**
1. **Specific procedures/services** with CPT codes where relevant (50-70 chars)
2. **Unique challenge solved** or value proposition (50-70 chars)
3. **Metrics or specific benefits** (40-60 chars)
4. **Additional differentiator** if space allows (30-50 chars)

**Example Template:**
```
Title: "[Specialty] Billing Services | [Unique Aspect] & [Procedure Type] RCM"
Description: "Comprehensive [specialty] billing for [specific procedure 1], [specific procedure 2], and [specific procedure 3] with [unique approach]. Expert handling of [specific challenge] including [specific detail (with CPT/modifier)]. [Unique benefit or credential]. [Metric: Increase/reduce X by Y%]."
```

### Key Differentiators to Include

**For Each Specialty, Highlight:**
1. **Most complex/high-value procedures**
2. **Specific CPT codes, modifiers, or J-codes**
3. **Unique billing challenges** (global periods, bundling, prior auth)
4. **Payer-specific requirements** (Medicare, Medicaid, commercial)
5. **Quantifiable results** (% revenue increase, denial reduction)
6. **Specialized certifications** or expertise
